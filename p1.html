Description

My partner Kristi:

My rag doll, named Amy, was soft all around. Soft to touch, I loved squeezing her chubby little limbs and round face. She had short, blonde, curly hair under her white bonnet hat, and she had round sky blue eyes. She wore a white top and light pink pants and shoes. Her bonnet hat, my favorite feature, was made of a frilly cotton material that looked lace.
