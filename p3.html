Project #3 Experimental Clock

Description

Do you want to use the Time Turner?

The inspiration of this clock is the Time Turner in Harry Potter. There are 2 ways to control time and a way to reset the time.

If you press and drag the mouse, the “wand” will insert magic power to change the time( when the tip of the wand is outside the clock, the time will lapse forward (and the further , the faster). When it is inside the clock, the time will lapse backward (also related to the distance).

And there is a faster way to go back your days. When your mouse moves to the spring of the clock( the small ring), the time will go back at a very high speed. When you’re going to let the time back to normal, you just need to click on the center of the clock.

Design Process

Code sourse link here

Because the time will not pass by regularly, so I can only use second(), minute() and hour() in setup(), and then I add time by add the value of variable in every loop of draw(). there are many steps to convert the developing variable into seemingly “time”.

And it is not easy to show the text time. We only need to use second() etc before, but I need to use many methods to express it, because the time in the canvas is not the time now.

The magic beam is an oscillator in math.

Reflection

The time that is subjective, personal, or even irrational organization can exists in our mind, but it is hard to express in code, because the change of our mood etc can not be shown by algorithm.
